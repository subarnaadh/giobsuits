Fit Guide

Not sure about your size? Here's a quick reference.

Jacket Size

The number is your chest measurement in inches. S = Short, R = Regular, L = Long torso.

Example: 40R = 40" chest, regular length.

Pant Size

Waist x Inseam in inches.

Example: 32x30 = 32" waist, 30" inseam.

Slim vs Regular

Slim fits closer to the body. Regular has more room throughout.
